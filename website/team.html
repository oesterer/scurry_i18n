The Scurry Team

Scurry is led by experts in fintech, compliance, engineering, and global-scale technology execution

Eric Kinney

Co-Founder & CEO

With over three decades of experience in financial services, Eric has worked across traditional banking, wealth technology, and fintech, specializing in operations, risk management, compliance, and financial crimes prevention. His career highlights include serving as a board-appointed Bank Secrecy Act Officer (BSAO) for four fintech companies, joining LendingClub as its seventh employee, co-founding AssetFort, and serving as BSA Officer at Oxygen and Slash. As a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) with the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE), he developed deep expertise in anti-money laundering controls and served as a Department of Justice witness in a major fintech case.

He holds Bachelor's degrees in Communication and Economics from the University of California San Diego (UCSD) and a Global Executive MBA from IE Business School in Madrid, Spain.

Beyond his professional pursuits, Eric is an accomplished endurance athlete, competing in long-distance running and open-water marathon swimming. He is also the author of the fintech thriller Scurry (Fraud Squad).

Andreas Oesterer

Co-Founder & CTO

An innovative and results-driven Technology Executive with over 20 years of experience, Andreas has led global engineering organizations and delivered transformative platforms across fintech, cloud, and enterprise systems. He combines strategic vision with deep technical expertise to scale high-performing teams, modernize platforms, and integrate complex systems supporting high-volume users and transactions, consistently driving operational resilience and measurable business impact through a bias for action. Andreas partners with executive leadership to shape technology strategy, guide risk and operational decisions, and operationalize roadmaps that align technical initiatives with business priorities.

Andreas holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from HTL Brugg-Windisch, Switzerland.

Outside work, Andreas is an avid tinkerer of robots, drones and 3D printed airplanes. He also supports the local high school robotics team.
